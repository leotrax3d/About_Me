Before you start: this benchmark deliberately runs each test flat-out — one core, then every core at once, then the GPU with a heavy shader. Your device may get warm, fans may spin up, and the page can briefly stop responding during the GPU run. Everything happens locally: nothing is uploaded and no cookies are used — your last results are kept only in this browser's local storage, and you can clear them below.
